Cribs NYC Project

About The Project

My Cribs NYC website is a full fledged application with frontend, backend, database, CRUD, user authentication, and email functionality. Its purpose is to provide a platform for the broker and agents to publish apartment listings, the customers to search for apartments and reach out to said agents, as well as search for individual rooms (a neccessity for most when moving to NYC). Key roles utilized in this application are the clients (users), and the real estate agents (admin access to upload apartment listings).



Technologies used - Python: Django, Postgres SQL, Google Maps API, Bootstrap, Javascript, Nginx, Gunicorn, Digital Ocean
