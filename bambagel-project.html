Bam Bagel Store Project

About The Project

The Bam Bagel Store is an e-commerce application with frontend, backend, database, CRUD, and paypal functionality. It was designed and built for a small boutique bagel shop in Syracuse, NY, to help the owner better manage bagel orders through and online portal where customers can buy and schedule times to pick up their product.



Technologies used - React, Redux, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Paypal API, Heroku
